Origins...

For centuries we were led to believe that these masks originated from Japan, their meaning all different as there are origin changes with the passage of time. Led to be Buddhist in origin…we were sorely mistaken. Their origins come from the great beyond not of this world. From a different dimension of which we still do not understand. In secret these masks have been hiding waiting for the perfect time to execute their plan. What we have seen in Japan's historical use of these masks is inspired by sightings of the mask scouts. The year is now 2022 and calamity has struck! The rift between our world and theirs has opened and they have started their invasion to take over our world. Masks flood the skys in vast arrays of color and gleam looking for their hosts to possess and roam the earth. The world has gone mad! Once a mask takes hold of a host they transform into what we call the Mad Mask. There is nowhere on earth you can hide where the masks can't find you. They hunger for a soul to possess. Are you next to transform into the Mad Mask?

About US

Why we do what we do! 

Goals

We are a community who specialize in Japanese mask inspired NFT collections. Our goal is to spread Japanese culture through our Mad Mask Club NFTs and community within the metaverse. Each mask has a symbolic meaning in Japan and each Mad Mask Club NFT you collect has its own story. All of our digital assets are 1 of 1 and are aimed to spread awareness of Japan's rich history and lore. We are not just planning on just releasing one collection. Our mission is to produce a series of collections, all based on different styles of Japanese masks throughout history. Each collection will contain 1 Legendary asset, 20 Epic assets, and 9,979 Rare assets.

Giving Back

We also want to give back to communities we support which is why 5% of our proceeds will go towards the United Nations Children's Fund (UNICEF) and 5% will go to the International Association for Suicide Prevention (IASP).
